Foundation Reflection

Three things I have learnt about myself

  • How important it is to take note of your mental space. Noticing my negative emotions can allow me to step outside them a bit and allow them to pass. Asking myself "am I feeling frustrated or Annoyed" has been very helpful in avoiding being stuck in those emotions for a long time.
  • I have learnt that my planning skills are better than I thought they were. Drawing up a reasonable plan for the week ahead has been very useful.
  • I actually haven't procrastinated too much during this week. And I think I have a better handle on my procrastinating tendencies that I had thought.

Empathy and Self Awareness in Learning

Having empathy and self awareness is hugely important in learning. By understanding that you will struggle, not understand everything immeditately, and fail a lot, it can allow you avoid negative mindsets. And having the self awareness that you are in a negative mindset and having empathy for yourself is very helpful for getting out of them and back into a mindset more conducive to learning. Being able to notice when I am feeling tired, frustrated, or distracted, has allowed me to step away for a bit and recenter myself and then come back and give the problems another shot.

Surprises

I thought human skills would be very woo-woo, spirtual but it was definitely more grounded than I thought it was going to be. And that I have already found like I've gotten a lot out of the metacognition, and thinking about my feelings at that moment and what has caused them.

Challenges

I think the most challenging aspect of the human skills, especially at first, was fully engaging with it. At first as I said above I was skeptical, and I definitely still am, although I am seeing some benefit in some of the exercises now.

Human Skills Importance

I think human skills are very important. Coding, like every other job, is done with a team of people. So being able to be a part of the team successfully and being able interact with others in a meaningful and useful manner is extremely important. As well as the interpersonal benefits there are also internal benefit. A more accurate idea of you rmindset and emotions is going to increase your emotional staying power then things get tough. And although learning these things does take time away from coding, I think any time taken away from learning code and instead learning these human skills, at the start of this journey, will pay dividends on the back end.
