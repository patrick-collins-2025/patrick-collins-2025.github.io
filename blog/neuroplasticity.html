Neuroplasticity & Growth Mindset

What is Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is how your brain is able to grow and reorganise itself. The ability for the brain to rewire connections, create new connections, or strengthen existing connections. Once thought to only exist in childhood, neuroplastisity exists in everyone and is an important part of learning.

What are the benefits

The ability for the brain to rewire, and strength its neural connections is important for learning new skills and absorbing new information. But that would happen with or without the knowledge of neuroplasticity, the importance of the knowledge of neuroplasticity is that by practice and repetition you are able to learn things that may have seemed beyond your ability before.

How can you increase your Neuroplasticity and use your Growth Mindset

Practice, repetition, connection of new knowledge to already existing knowledge and skill, all these things will increase and strengthen the neural conncetions in your brain, making further learning of skills and information in that area easier and increasing ability.
